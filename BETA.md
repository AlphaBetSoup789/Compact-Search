# Compact — Free Beta

Compact is currently in **free public beta**. API keys issued during this period are free for the duration of the beta stage.

---

## What "free beta" means

- Your beta API key works from the day you receive it until the beta stage ends.
- No usage limits are imposed during beta beyond fair-use abuse prevention.
- There is no automatic conversion to a paid plan — if and when pricing is introduced, you will be notified in advance and given time to decide whether to continue.

---

## What happens after beta

Pricing and tiers have not been finalized. Possibilities being considered include:

- A continued free tier for certain clients or use patterns
- Usage-based or subscription pricing for others

Nothing is decided yet. The goal is to keep Compact accessible to individual developers and open-source projects while covering the infrastructure costs that make it reliable.

When decisions are made they will be announced here and via the channels below before any key stops working.

## Make your key last

The best way to ensure Compact stays free or stays affordable is to help it grow:

- **Contribute field trials** — edge cases, version-specific fixes, undocumented behavior. See [CONTRIBUTION-SYSTEM.md](CONTRIBUTION-SYSTEM.md).
- **Tag [@usecompact](https://twitter.com/usecompact)** with `#compactfieldtrial` when you share a finding.
- **Spread the word** — every developer who uses and contributes makes the cache more valuable for everyone.

A larger, more active community is the strongest argument for keeping costs low.
